Chest exercises :

Workout 1: incline chest press

The exercise involves pressing dumbbells from the chest by extending the arms up, while sitting at a 30 to 45 incline.

Workout 2: fly machine

Chest Fly Machines primarily target the pectoral muscles, promoting muscle isolation, enhancing upper body strength, and facilitating muscle engagement and development.

Workout 3: dumbbell chest press

The dumbbell bench press helps to build strength and stability in the pectorals, deltoids, and triceps. It is an effective upper body movement that makes a suitable addition to your workout routine if you want to build strength or muscle mass.
